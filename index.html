Future residents of DAMAC Bay will be able to choose a unit from 1-3 bedroom apartments ranging in size from 780 sqft offering views of Dubai Marina, the Arabian Gulf, Ain Dubai and surrounding areas. Luxury units will occupy 30 floors, while 8 floors will be dedicated to ultra-luxury residences. Three towers will be connected by a podium and a sky garden. A swimming pool will be located on the podium of the building, and direct access to the beach and promenade will be offered to residents. As the project will be branded by Cavalli, occupants will also be provided with more upcoming premium amenities letting them to experience harmonious lifestyle in one of the sought-after areas of the emirate.

Location

Nestled in the developing area of Dubai Harbour. Due to the position of the area at the intersection of the iconic Palm Jumeirah and Bluewaters, residents of luxury seafront apartments will easily get to such famous sights of Dubai as Jumeirah Beach, Ain Dubai and more.
